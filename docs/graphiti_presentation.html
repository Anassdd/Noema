Noema · Graphiti

Text goes in. A temporal knowledge graph comes out.

Every page of every document becomes an “episode”. Graphiti reads it, pulls out the entities and the facts connecting them, and folds them into one living graph — where a fact that changes is marked out of date, never erased.

Every fact is bi-temporal. Two clocks: when it was true in the world (valid / invalid) and when the system learned or superseded it (created / expired). This is why Graphiti can reason about time — not just store facts, but know which held when.
Because the world changes — invalidate, don't delete
